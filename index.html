Privacy Policy for Smart Shop

Last Updated: [Current Date]

Welcome to Smart Shop (developed by Yasir Digital World). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your business data is secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services (POS, Inventory, Khata Book), we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Shop Name, Phone Number, and Email Address used for account registration.
  • Business Data: Inventory details (Product names, prices, quantity), Sales records, and Bill history.
  • Khata (Ledger) Data: Customer names, phone numbers, and transaction amounts (credit/debit) that you voluntarily enter into the system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for the functionality of the app:

  • To manage your stock and inventory in real-time.
  • To generate bills and calculate daily/monthly profits.
  • To maintain your digital ledger (Khata Book).
  • To authenticate your login and secure your account.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Authentication. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google Firebase: For backend database, hosting, and user authentication.
  • WhatsApp: Our app allows you to share bills or payment reminders via WhatsApp. We do not read or store your WhatsApp messages; we simply redirect to the WhatsApp application installed on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or business data to third parties. Your data is yours and is strictly used for your business management within the app.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your business data at any time.
  • Edit or delete products, sales, and khata entries directly from the app.
  • Request the deletion of your account by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
